Common Baking Errors

  • Problem: Cake is too dense.
    Solution: Ensure your leavening agents (baking soda/powder) are fresh and accurately measured.
  • Problem: Cookies spread too much.
    Solution: Chill the dough before baking and use the correct flour type.
  • Problem: Muffins are dry.
    Solution: Don’t overmix the batter and check your oven temperature.
  • Problem: Cake sinks in the middle.
    Solution: Avoid opening the oven door too early and ensure your batter isn’t overmixed.
  • Problem: Bread doesn’t rise.
    Solution: Check that your yeast is active and your liquid isn’t too hot or too cold.
