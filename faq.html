Frequently Asked Questions

You can use flaxseed meal mixed with water, applesauce, or mashed bananas as effective egg substitutes in vegan baking.

Use ingredients like coconut milk, vegan yogurt, or a splash of vegetable oil to add moisture to your cakes.

Unsweetened almond milk, soy milk, or oat milk are excellent choices for baking as they have a neutral flavor and good consistency.
